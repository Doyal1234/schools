Know about Kendriya Vidyalaya Garden Reach, Kolkata: Board & Curriculum, Admission Procedure, Fees & Fee Structure, Contact Details, Facilities and Ranking, Rating and Reviews. Kendriya Vidyalaya Garden Reach Kolkata, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from I to XII. It is an Hindi, English Medium school. The school is located in Garden Reach area of Kolkata, West Bengal. Kendriya Vidyalaya Garden Reach was established in 1988.


Library

It was established in 1672

The library of KV, Lalitpur is growing day by day through acquisition of required books. Currently the library has 1948 books. The library is also equipped with computer system, projector, BCR and bar code printer. For automation the library uses e-Granthalaya software. Library periods are used to bring students to the library for reading and issue/return of the books from circulation counter. The library aims to promote reading habits in students as well as staff of KV, Lalitpur. The library holdings are arranged subject wise for quick retrieval of books. Biographies of several eminent politicians, national heroes, scientists etc are made available to students. Five different Newspapers and twenty one magazines are available in library for staff and students. Book borrow cards are used for issue return to students and register is maintained for issue/return to staff. Facility of internet is also available in the library. A Library Orientation Program is conducted at the beginning of new session for the students of Class Six. Various activity based programs are conducted to create skills among the students. What's new

  • New School Canteen

    School canteen facility aims to provide children of working parents to buy nutritious and healthy food at competitive prices during the school day. Meals are either cooked on the premises or bought from approved restaurants and food outlets. Pupils who cannot take full breakfast from home may bring some form of healthy snack with them for break time. Pupils can also buy snacks items and fruits from the snacks counter. Parents shall send canteen food order slip or coupon through the pupil for availing canteen services. Payments can be made daily, weekly or monthly. There is a choice of menu that operates on a rota basis during the week. If you would like to learn more about the menu and payment system, please contact the Catering Supervisor.

  • New Crafting Room

    Art and craft education is an integral part of student's learning and living to stimulate their imagination and to stir up a lot of artistic and inventive ideas. It nurtures creativity of students with necessary equipment to sensitize their aesthetic qualities. It will enhance child’s individual’s craftsmanship, giving them an exposure to various types of craft and art. School focuses on sparking students creativity by exploring 2-Dimensional and 3-dimensional art, painting, madhubani art, warli art, line painting, silhouette painting, newspaper painting, mosaic painting, dot painting, one-perspective painting. Craft is a fun way of making students learn about making use of household objects into work of art. Students are encouraged to perform different forms of craft work such as Paper folding, rangoli making, Diwali decoration, craft with CD, diya decoration, wall hangings, envelope making, rakhi making, 3-D birds etc. All these activities enhance child’s individual craftsmanship, giving them an exposure to various types of craft and art. These are the opportunities that a child gets to work on them and find a way to develop confidence, a sense of competence, vivid imagination and bloom with creativity.
